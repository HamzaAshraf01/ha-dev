Globus, the renowned and luxurious Swiss department store chain turned to our agency with the aim of developing a creative idea for the launch of their new store in Saint Gallen. We took the opportunity offered by the brand's fascinating historic building to create a perfect replica of the building. This elegant paper replica, more than 3 meters tall, became the central focus o f our launch campaign, becoming the protagonist of many digital and traditional communication assets.

Don't miss the backstage video where you can observe all the construction phases of the paper palace. The Method for Globus: Launch video Key Visual Production Main showcase Video Digital Out Of Home Product shooting for the Magazine Paper invitation for the launch event Web Banner Discover the passion and precision that fueled this wonderful project by watching the video by backstage, an adventure that will take you into the heart of the creative process.
